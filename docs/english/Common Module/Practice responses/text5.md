# Text 5 - Magazine Cover

## Explain ONE way that text 5 explores the anomolies and/or incosistencies in human behaviour

Text 5 explores how children will play games about going outside instead of just going outside themselves. This inconsistency in desires invites the reader to think on what they really desire. In this case, the kids wish to play games online rather than going outside physically. To older generations, this may seem as an anomoly of behaviour as they would hate staying inside and instead would love to spend time outside, which highly contradicts the current state of youth.
